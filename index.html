Quick presentation of our website

The main objective of this website is to make discover Angers by a new and fresh view. You will find different places to chill with friends or to have fun, cool spots and also the principle historical places to visit.
The opinions on places and spaces presented in this site are our own, it is important for us that these opinions are honest and represent what we think as a student. Therefore it is possible that our opinions differ from the opinions that can be found on the internet.
